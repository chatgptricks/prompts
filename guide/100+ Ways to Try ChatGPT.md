# Project-Based Workflows to Try in ChatGPT (2026 edition):

**1\. Set up a "Client Research Hub" shared project**  
 Prompt: Create a shared project for prospect research. Upload your ICP definition, competitor analysis, and past successful client profiles. Ask team members to add new prospects and get AI-generated research briefs, outreach strategies, and qualification scorecards that stay consistent across your entire sales team.

**2\. Build a "Campaign Command Center" for marketing teams**  
 Prompt: Establish a shared project called "Q2 Product Launch" with brand guidelines, previous campaign performance data, and target audience personas. Have different team members contribute content ideas, ad copy, and landing page concepts while maintaining unified messaging and tone across all deliverables.

**3\. Create an "Operations Playbook" for recurring processes**  
 Prompt: Set up a project with your SOPs, checklists, and process documentation. Ask ChatGPT to generate step-by-step workflows for common scenarios, risk assessment templates for new initiatives, and post-mortem frameworks that reference your actual policies and procedures.

**4\. Launch a "Support Knowledge Base" for customer service teams**  
 Prompt: Build a shared project with FAQ documents, product manuals, and sample ticket resolutions. Team members can instantly get drafted responses to customer inquiries that cite your actual help documentation, ensuring consistent, accurate support while dramatically reducing response time.

**5\. Develop a "Financial Analysis Center" for data-driven decisions**  
 Prompt: Create a project with historical financial data, budget templates, and KPI definitions. Upload monthly reports and ask ChatGPT to identify trends, flag anomalies, and generate executive summaries that automatically reference your specific business metrics and benchmarks.

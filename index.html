ChatGPTricks

ChatGPT 5.5 can now create a presentation in just minutes

Here are the 7 prompts that you should try

1

Full Presentation Builder

Act as a world-class presentation creator. Create a complete, slide-by-slide presentation on the topic: [insert topic]

Include title slides, key points, examples, data, metaphors, visuals suggestions, and a closing call-to-action

Structure it like a TED-level presentation with storytelling flow

2

Deep Research Presentation

Create a full presentation on [insert topic] using deep research

Include real statistics, case studies, expert quotes, practical insights, and examples

Make each slide educational, engaging, and backed by credible sources. Provide references at the end

3

Simplified Explanation

Build a presentation on [insert topic] designed for beginners.

Break down complex concepts into simple explanations, analogies, and step-by-step logic.

Make the content so clear that even a 10-year-old can understand the full topic.

4

Business Pitch Presentation

Create a complete investor-style pitch deck for my business idea: [insert idea]

Include problem, solution, market size, product features, business model, competitive advantage, financial projections, team slide, and closing pitch script

5

The Storytelling Master

Create a complete presentation on [insert topic] using storytelling structure:

Hook → Conflict → Journey → Insight → Transformation → Call to Action.

Make slides emotional, memorable, and persuasive. Add speaker notes with delivery guidance

6

Corporate Presentation

Create a formal, corporate-style presentation on [insert topic]

Include professional tone, structured bullet points, charts, graphs, SWOT analysis, trends, forecasts, and data-backed recommendations

Make it suitable for boardrooms

7

Complete Presentation + Script Combo

Create a fully ready presentation on [insert topic], AND write a complete spoken script I can read while presenting

Ensure the script matches the tone, pacing, and emotional flow of the slides

Include pauses, emphasis, storytelling cues, and delivery instructions

Pro Tips for the Best Results

  • Iterate and Refine: Don't settle for the first draft. Ask ChatGPT to expand on specific slides or change the tone.
  • Add Your Personal Touch: Use the AI-generated content as a foundation, but inject your own stories and experiences to make it authentic.
  • Visuals Matter: Pair these prompts with tools like DALL-E 3 or Midjourney to generate stunning, custom images for your slides.
  • Practice Delivery: The best script won't work without good delivery. Use the "Complete Presentation + Script Combo" to rehearse pacing.
